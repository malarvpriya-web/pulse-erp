# CUSTOMER RISK MODEL — Phase 49F-15

## Risk Dimensions

Each customer is assessed on 5 risk axes independently. Each axis is rated: **low | medium | high | critical**.

### 1. Revenue Loss Risk
- **low**: Revenue growing or stable, regular orders
- **medium**: Revenue flat for 12+ months, few orders
- **high**: Revenue declining year-over-year
- **critical**: No orders in 12 months

### 2. Payment Default Risk
- **low**: No overdue invoices
- **medium**: 1–30 days overdue invoices exist
- **high**: 31–60 days overdue
- **critical**: 90+ days overdue invoices outstanding

### 3. Project Escalation Risk
- **low**: All projects delivered on time
- **medium**: 1 project delayed or 1 active overdue
- **high**: 2+ delayed OR active overdue projects
- **critical**: Project failures OR 3+ active overdue

### 4. Service Escalation Risk
- **low**: All tickets resolved, no critical open
- **medium**: 1–2 open tickets
- **high**: 5+ open tickets
- **critical**: Critical-priority tickets unresolved

### 5. AMC Non-Renewal Risk
- **low**: Active AMC, not expiring
- **medium**: AMC expiring within 90 days
- **high**: AMC about to lapse and no active coverage
- **critical**: AMC lapsed, no renewal initiated

## Recommended Actions

| Risk Level | Response Time  | Owner              |
|------------|----------------|--------------------|
| Low        | Normal cadence | Account Manager    |
| Medium     | Within 2 weeks | Account Manager    |
| High       | Within 3 days  | Sales/Service Lead |
| Critical   | Within 24 hours| VP / CEO           |

## Overall Risk Profile
The highest severity across all 5 dimensions defines the customer's overall risk level displayed in the Customer 360 header and Health Dashboard.
